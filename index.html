Description:

Introducing the innovative, low-cost walk-behind turf aerator from PlanetAir. By swapping tines, these aerators can create deep vertical cuts or pull cores and process them right inside the machine.

The PlanetAir PA29 Aerator offers the following features:

  • Patented planetary gear drive system creates a small incision while stirring soil beneath the cut, leaving little disruption to the surface
  • Lightweight, corrosion-resistant aluminum housing
  • Self-propelled with three speed low-maintenance transmission
  • Low-cost, high performance machine with quick return on investment
  • Briggs & Stratton Engine
  • Includes roller scrapers
  • (22) 1/8" Shatter Knife Tine Included
